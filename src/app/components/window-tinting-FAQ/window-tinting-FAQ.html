Is car window tinting legal?

Yes absolutely! There is no law governing the tints of the rear windows of the car, however the law does state that the front windows must let through 70% of light and the windscreen must let through 75% of light. Our staff can guide you through this further.

Does my car have window tint fitted to the inside or outside?

All window tint is fitted to the inside of the car, although you may see in some photos us working on the outside, this is due to the ease of cutting and shaping the film on the outside of the glass first before you window tint is fitted to the inside of the car.

Do you need to take my car apart to tint it?

No, absolutely not! A skilled window tint installer, such as our staff, can tint a car without needing to take the door cards off or remove a fixed parcel shelf.
