About us

Established in 2012 Oxford Performance Autos is Oxfordshire’s premier wheel refurbishment and window tinting centre. We are conveniently located between Oxford and Witney. All our work is fully ensured and guaranteed as well as delivered with a quick turnaround for a great price. We are so confident that you won’t find a better service we offer a price guarantee for our alloy wheel refurbishments and window tints. If you can find a like for like service within 30 miles, we will beat it. We don’t just offer fantastic car alloy wheel refurbishments and window tinting, we also offer great value tyres, ECU remapping, vinyl wrapping and de-chroming.
